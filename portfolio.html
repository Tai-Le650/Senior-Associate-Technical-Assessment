Project

01 2026 Godot · GDScript

Ecosystem Simulator

An educational, web-based sandbox game built in Godot 4.6 with GDScript, where players draw terrains and place plants and animals to see how their choices shape an ecosystem over time. It includes multiple game modes: Free Play, Pre-Lab, Walk-Through simulation, and Explore Planets; plus an AI helper bot with text/speech support, as well as post-simulation analysis, to help high school and undergraduate students learn STEM concepts. Quizzes at the end of Pre-Lab and Walk-Through, sim scrubbing, and a 3D sandbox round it out.

(Currently using free API calls for Goggle Gemini Flash 2.5 lite and open source text to voice for the audio talk back on the AI helper bot. Rate limits on data APIs that are used in the Explore Planets game mode.)
